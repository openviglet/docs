# Enterprise Search

Turing indexes your content into the search engine you already operate — **Apache
Solr**, **Elasticsearch** or an **embedded Lucene** index — and exposes it through a
single query API. Facets, locale-aware analyzers, autocomplete, spell-check and
currency/number localization ship in the box, so you get the depth enterprise
catalogs and knowledge bases need without stitching libraries together. Everything
runs on your infrastructure; your content never leaves your network.

Define a site's fields in a **versioned manifest** and Turing converges the live
schema to match, flagging breaking changes before they run. Turn on **hybrid
ranking** and results are reordered with Reciprocal Rank Fusion over BM25 and
per-site vectors — semantic relevance without giving up exact-match precision —
while a coverage view shows exactly which fields your documents actually populate.
